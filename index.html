It's PORTABLE: you can move it

dragging a thing from one container to another

For dragging between places, you need at least two places.

Your mission is to drag item 1 from place 1 to place 2

  • item 1
  1. restore custom console
  2. modify console to call datafy (because it's a f'n repl)
  3. listen for drag:start events
  4. log event
  5. datafy Event
  6. log datafied event
  7. refine datafy for DragEvent
  8. repeat for drag:drag
  9. repeat for drag:over
  10. repeat for drag:end
  11. reify drag
  12. Next: selectable: reify mark
  13. reify: indication (hover)
  14. reify: indication/selection (touch)
  15. reify: unicode

Use case. Tear off a link, boom, you've made a layer. A layer where the selected link now has the mic, the point of view.

I mean, there might be more particular things to do with any given term. You may need to drop it on a “dead zone” to reliably make a layer. The drop zone should be located where that is the most obvious thing to do. In the dead zone generally targets are dormant and unresponsive to the dragged thing. But yeah. That alone.
