About DOM elements one thing is sure: they are real. They are as real as anything in the world of computing, in that people's time is devoted to them. Moreover, most people are, right now, in possession of ready tools for their discovery and inspection! Not to mention intervention! So, while all elements are portable, consider the `article` element as a special agent of portability. Article indicates a disposition, an embedded intent to be shared, to be picked up and moved around. Indeed, what other kind of thing is there? Yet most elements we would not expect to survive out of context. Article represents a (more or less) standalone thing, that you may carry anywhere and leave anywhere.
